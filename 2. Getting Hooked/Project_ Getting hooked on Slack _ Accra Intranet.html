More Info

Today, you are connecting with your peers using Slack.

In slack we will find the following cohort channels. The x is your cohort number

Your cohort

  • #cohort_x You can reach out to your peers here. Also, use the @mentor tag from here to get help from your mentor.
  • #cohort_x_announcements Announcements from the staff will be placed here.
  • #cohort_x_review You can post reviews here.
  • #abc_0123 Your cohort for your location will be housed here. abc is the three letter code for your location, and 0123 the month and year when you started.

To aid one another

In case you’d like to ask a question to peers or to the staff, kindly use #ask_a_question and find the appropriate channel there:

  • #ask_a_c_question - to ask all questions regarding C programming language
  • #ask_a_python_question - To ask all questions regarding Python programming language.
  • #ask_a_javascript_question - To ask all questions regarding JavaScript, ReactJS, NodeJS etc.
  • #ask_a_sql_question - To ask questions regarding SQL.
  • #ask_a_dev-ops_question - To ask all questions regarding DevOps and Shell, NGINX, HAProxy, etc.
  • #ask_a_html_or_css_question - To ask all questions regarding HTML and CSS.
  • #ask_a_general_question - To ask all questions which are not programming language related question e.g. GIT, Sandboxes etc.
  • #ask_a_program_question - To ask all questions related the program itself. e.g. Foundations, Specialization or Support questions.
  • #ask_a_question_about_the_room - To ask questions related to the Room.

To join any of the above channels, just click on the specific channel link.
